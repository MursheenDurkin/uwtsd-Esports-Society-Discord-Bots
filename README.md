## The Bots

### Spot — Server Management
Named after the Boston Dynamics Spot robot dog living in the Robotics Lab at UWTSD Swansea. Handles verification, moderation, announcements, free game alerts, and in-character interactions.

### Atlas — Esports Operations
Named after the Boston Dynamics Atlas humanoid robot. Handles rank tracking, player registration, team management, tournaments, events, VOD review, and statistics.

## Ownership & Continuity Policy

Both bots were designed, built, and are maintained solely by **Barney** — President of UWTSD Esports Society at the time of creation.

- **Ownership** remains with Barney at all times. This does not transfer as part of any society handover.
- Incoming leadership is welcome to continue using both bots — they will simply continue to run as they always have.
- Access credentials, hosting, and codebases are not part of the society's assets.
- **Neither bot is included in the society handover. Full stop.**
